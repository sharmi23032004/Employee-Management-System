Leave Policy

1. Leave Entitlement

All full-time employees are entitled to the following annual leave:

  • Casual Leave: 12 days
  • Sick Leave: 8 days
  • Privilege Leave: 4 days

2. Application Process

Leave applications must be submitted through the EMS system and require manager approval. Emergency leaves should be communicated immediately via phone or email.

3. Notice Period

  • Casual Leave: Minimum 2 days advance notice
  • Sick Leave: Immediate notification required
  • Privilege Leave: Minimum 1 week advance notice

4. Documentation

Medical certificates are mandatory for sick leaves exceeding 2 consecutive days. Supporting documents should be attached to the leave application.

5. Leave Balance

Unused casual and sick leave cannot be carried forward. Privilege leave may be carried forward up to 50% with manager approval.

6. Public Holidays

Public holidays falling during approved leave period will not be counted against leave balance.
