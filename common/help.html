Frequently Asked Questions

Attendance

How do I mark my daily attendance?

To mark your attendance:

  1. Navigate to My Attendance from the sidebar
  2. Click on Check In button when you arrive
  3. Click Check Out when leaving for the day
  4. Your attendance will be automatically recorded with timestamps

Note: Grace period of 15 minutes is allowed for late check-ins.

What if I forgot to mark attendance?

If you forgot to mark attendance, contact your manager or HR department. They can manually update your attendance records. Provide the date and approximate working hours.

Can I view my attendance history?

Yes! Go to My Attendance and use the calendar view to see your complete attendance history, including check-in/out times, working hours, and any late arrivals.

Leave Management

How do I apply for leave?

To apply for leave:

  1. Go to Leave section
  2. Click Apply Leave button
  3. Select leave type (Casual, Sick, Privilege, etc.)
  4. Choose start and end dates
  5. Add reason for leave
  6. Submit for manager approval

How many leaves am I entitled to?

Standard leave entitlements per year:

  • Casual Leave: 12 days
  • Sick Leave: 12 days
  • Privilege Leave: 15 days

You can view your current leave balance in the Leave section.

Can I cancel my leave request?

Yes, you can cancel pending leave requests before they are approved. Go to Leave History, find your request, and click the Cancel button. Once approved, contact your manager to cancel.

Payroll & Payslips

When will I receive my salary?

Salaries are processed on the last working day of each month. The amount is credited to your registered bank account by the 1st or 2nd of the following month.

How can I download my payslip?

To download your payslip:

  1. Navigate to Payslips section
  2. Select the month you need
  3. Click the Download button
  4. Payslip will be downloaded as a PDF

What deductions are made from my salary?

Standard deductions include:

  • Provident Fund (PF): 12% of basic salary
  • Professional Tax: As per state regulations
  • Income Tax (TDS): Based on tax slab
  • Leave deductions: For unpaid leave days

All deductions are detailed in your monthly payslip.

Profile & Account

How do I update my personal information?

You can update your email, phone number, and address in the My Profile section. For changes to your name, date of birth, or bank details, please contact HR with supporting documents.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Enter your current password
  3. Enter new password (min 8 characters, with uppercase & number)
  4. Confirm new password and save

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link within 5 minutes.
